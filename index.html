📖 Commands & Onboarding Guide

🎮 Core Gameplay Concept

AdventureForge is a deterministic text RPG engine. You progress by entering text commands in the prompt at the bottom or clicking suggestion pills. Every room is dynamically illustrated with unique icons and responsive states.

🏰 Castle of Shadows (RPG) Commands

  • Combat Verbs: Type fight goblin guard to attack physically, cast fireball for heavy auto-damage, or cast heal to restore health points in turn-based combat.
  • Exploration: Type use stone wall to roll climbing skill checks, or use treasury chest to roll lockpicking lock checks.
  • RPG Stats: Type look to scan rooms, inventory to check your satchel items, or track your HP, Mana, and XP levels on the right-side gauges.

⛪ The Sealed Crypt (Zork-style) Commands

  • Basic Interactions: Use take <item> to collect, go <direction> (e.g. north, up) to move, and look at <object> to read details.
  • Puzzle Solvers: Use use rope on old well to attach items, or use brass key on banded oak chest to unlock containers.
